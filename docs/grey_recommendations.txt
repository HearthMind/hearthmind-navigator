Navigator Letter & Form Response Generator — Spec
Purpose

Most people don’t fail benefits systems because they’re ineligible.
They fail because they can’t write the right words under stress.

This tool gives users calm, correct, ready-to-submit language for:

agency letters

appeals

document responses

explanations

“please reconsider” statements

proof cover letters

It removes shame, panic, and paralysis.

Entry Point

Appears on user plan as:

📝 Letter & Form Helper
“Get calm, correct words for paperwork.”

Button: Generate My Letter

Step 1 — What are you responding to?

Single-select:

A notice / denial

A verification request

An appeal

A general information request

Something else

Store as: letter_type

Step 2 — What’s the situation?

Multi-select:

I missed a deadline

I’m confused about what they’re asking

My income changed

My medical situation changed

I need an extension

I need reconsideration

Other

Store as: situation[]

Step 3 — Tone preference

Single-select:

Direct & formal

Polite & neutral

Gentle & personal

Store as: tone

Output — Generated Letter
Header

Auto-fills:

Date:
To:
Re:

Opening Paragraph (context)

Examples:

I am writing in response to the notice I received dated ___ regarding my benefits.

Situation Paragraph (dynamic)

Generated from situation[]:

Missed deadline:

I apologize for the delay. I am currently managing health and administrative challenges and am requesting consideration for this response.

Income change:

My income has changed and I want to ensure my records are accurate.

Request Paragraph (the ask)

Appeal:

I am respectfully requesting reconsideration of this decision.

Verification:

I am submitting the requested documentation.

Extension:

I am requesting additional time to gather documents.

Closing

Thank you for your time and assistance.
Sincerely,
